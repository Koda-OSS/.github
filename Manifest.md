**Koda OSS** is the open-source home for the Koda ecosystem. We provide the fundamental libraries, language specifications, and tooling that power our platform, enabling developers to build, extend, and integrate with Koda standards.

</div>

## Core Pillars

- **Open Standards:** We build tools that don't lock you in. Anyone is free to use our designs to create their own compatible systems.
- **Building in Public:** We believe software gets better when people work together. Our discussions, issues, and ideas happen out in the open.
- **Reliable Reference Code:** We provide high-quality implementations that are lightweight, easy to read, and clear to follow.
- **Community First:** We welcome contributions of all shapes and sizes. New ideas and feedback help keep our tools healthy and relevant.

---

## How We Build Software

Our approach to engineering is straightforward. No matter what kind of project we are working on, we try to follow three basic goals:

### 1. Fast

We design tools to run as efficiently as possible, keeping performance in mind from the start.

- We focus on writing lean code that minimizes resource usage.
- We avoid unnecessarily large or bulky external dependencies to keep our projects lightweight.

### 2. Clean

We believe software should be simple to understand and easy to extend.

- We avoid rigid rules that restrict how you can use our tools in your own environment.
- We keep our project structures clean and readable, making them straightforward to integrate with other setups.

### 3. Secure

We build implementations that protect systems from breaking under unexpected conditions.

- We prefer simple, predictable architectures over complicated and nested configurations.
- We use clear validation rules that catch errors early, preventing small mistakes from causing larger runtime issues.

## Keeping Things Clear

We maintain a strict boundary between **Koda OSS** (our public, community-focused tools) and **Koda-sh** (our proprietary products). By focusing our open-source efforts purely on reliable utility libraries and standards, we ensure developers always have a stable foundation to build upon.
